StrideShow is a web+mobile presentation web application

Download the mobile app

HIGHLY RECOMMENDED but optional: The app is a significant part of a StrideShow presentation

The rest of this tutorial involves the mobile app

On the app's main page, click the "Proceed to Demo" button

In the demo activity,

1. Tap the StrideShow icon on the menu toolbar at the top of the phone screen to reveal the connection fragment

2. Enter the generated room number that you see under "Socket Status" (you won't be able to see it in fullscreen mode) into your
mobile device and press "Enter Room"

3. Wait for the "Mobile Status" on top (just beside Socket Status) to indicate that your mobile device has connected

Look back at your mobile device.

Close the connection fragment by tapping the StrideShow icon again.

Tap the "Instructions" presentation to control it through your phone.

You can now swipe left and right on the view pager to traverse the slideshow on this browser. Continue controlling this presentation on mobile

Switch to full screen by clicking the button on the bottom right of this slideshow container

On your mobile device, try the laser pointer by dragging the circular enclosed plus sign around
